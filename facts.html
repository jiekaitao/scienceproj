Did you know?

-Thyroid cancer has the highest 10-year survival rate out of any kind of cancer at 94.6%
-One of the fastest growing cases at over 20,000 new cases per year.
-Women are up to four times more likely to get thyroid cancer than men.
-Thyroid cancer is the most common endocrine cancer, meaning relating to glands.
-Two out of three people diagnosed are younger than 55.
- Thyroid cancer is completely asymptomatic in the beginning, then the patient starts to develop a lump.
